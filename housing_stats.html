Distribution of Housing Prices by Number of Bedrooms (County and Year)

Distribution Shape

Availability of homes by price range show a fairly normal distribution with respect to number of bedrooms. As prices rise significantly, a new distribution of homes in a higher range of bedroom numbers appears.

Most Represented Price Range

Homes in the price range of $1M-$2M challenged or outsold homes in any other price range across six out of the seven counties for all of the included years, disrupting the distribution trend.

Additional Insights

Year 2008 saw the lowest home sales of all years included. Prince George's County is the only county to not have distribution-upsetting home sales in the $1M-$2M range.
