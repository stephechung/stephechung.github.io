COVID-19 Impact

The initial onset of COVID-19 impacted the spring market purchases April-May 2020. However, the market recovered to match the previous years performance.

Post-Pandemic Demand

The effect of the pandemic on housing demand resulted in 11.2% additional home sales compared to 2020. Additional considerations include limited inventory and shortened listing time on the market.
